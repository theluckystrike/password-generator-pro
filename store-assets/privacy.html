Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

Introduction

Password Generator Pro ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Summary: We collect absolutely nothing. Your passwords are generated locally and never transmitted anywhere. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking.

Data Collection

Password Generator Pro does NOT collect any data:

  • We do not collect passwords you generate
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not collect browsing history
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not use cookies or tracking

Password Generation

All passwords are generated locally in your browser using the Web Crypto API. No passwords are ever transmitted over the internet or stored on any server.

Local Storage

The extension stores only your preferences (password length and character options) locally using Chrome's storage API. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is never transmitted externally
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

Password history is kept in session memory only and is automatically cleared when you close the browser.

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Used to save your preferences (password length, character options) locally. This is the only permission required.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Security

Password Generator Pro uses the Web Crypto API (crypto.getRandomValues) to generate cryptographically secure random passwords. This is the same technology used by security professionals.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any information from anyone.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support page or visit our website.
